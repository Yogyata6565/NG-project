FAQ

Is it really free? Yes! The program is 100% free ‚Äî including education, hostel, food, and internet. NavGurukul believes that quality education should be accessible to all, regardless of financial background.
Who can apply? Youth from underserved or low-income backgrounds, aged 17 and above, who are passionate to learn and grow, can apply. No prior experience is needed, just the dedication to change your life.
What after the program? After completing the program, students receive support for job placements, mentorship, freelancing, and even higher studies. Our alumni work in top tech companies and continue to uplift others.

SARJAPUR CAMPUS
NavGurukul Sarjapur is a residential campus that provides free education, food, and accommodation to underprivileged youth in India. Through a one-year program, students learn full-stack web development, English communication, and essential life skills in a collaborative environment. The campus promotes peer learning, self-paced study, and leadership to help students transform their lives and secure meaningful careers in the tech industry.

NavGurukul Admission Portal

A mission to provide free education and career opportunities for underserved youth.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Minimum Education: You should have completed 10th grade or dropped out after 10th. Higher education is not required.
  • Age Limit: You must be 17 years old.
  • Family Income: The annual income should be below ₹3 lakh per year.
  • Availability: You should be ready to stay full-time at the campus or regularly attend online classes.
  • Commitment: Must be serious, disciplined, and committed to learning throughout the course.
  • Inclusion: Girls, trans youth, and marginalized communities are strongly encouraged to apply.

Programs Offered

  • SOP – School of Programming
    Duration: 18 Months (Full-time)
    What You Learn: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Git, logical thinking, problem solving
    Career Outcome: Software Developer, Web Developer, Internships in tech companies.
  • SOB – School of Business
    Duration: 8 Months (Full-time)
    What You Learn: English Communication, Typing, Email Writing, Google Docs, Sheets, Presentations, Soft Skills
    Career Outcome: Jobs in customer support, admin, operations, and BPOs.
  • SOD – School of Data Analytics
    Duration: 6–9 Months
    What You Learn: Canva, Photoshop, video editing, storytelling, basic digital marketing
    Career Outcome: Freelance designer, content creator, or creative internships.

Enrollment Steps

  • Step 1: Fill Application Form
    Go to the official website Navgurukul.org and submit your personal, educational, and family background details.
  • Step 2: Take Entrance Test
    You will receive a link to an online test that checks your basic English, logical thinking, and motivation.
  • Step 3: Interview
    Selected students will be called for a video interview to understand their goals and passion for learning.
  • Step 4: Confirmation
    If selected, you will get a confirmation message with instructions to join the program.
  • Step 5: Join Campus or Batch
    You can join a physical campus or online learning batch depending on your preference.
  • Step 6: Start Learning
    Congratulations! Your journey begins with mentorship, projects, and career preparation.
